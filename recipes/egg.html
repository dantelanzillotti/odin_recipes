Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a classic dish made by beating eggs, then gently cooking them while stirring over low to medium heat. The result is a soft, creamy, and slightly fluffy texture, though the consistency can range from loose and custardy to firm and well-set, depending on preference and technique.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and add in your seasonings to taste.
  2. Add the butter to a pan and heat on high unti very hot
  3. Pour in your whisked eggs and mix until desired consistacy.
  4. Serve!
